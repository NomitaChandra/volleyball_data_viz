About the Data

Data Source

We collected data on player performance, team standings, and match outcomes from official Big Ten conference records and statistics databases to ensure the accuracy and reliability of our sources for meaningful analysis.

Data Timeline

Our analysis covers data from 2016 to 2022, focusing on the more recent years to apply for today's context. Over those years we collected 17 statistics for each team.

Purpose

Our goal is to empower coaches and schools with a tool that enhances decision-making in recruiting and coaching by analyzing player performance to set strategic goals and recruit players based on their playing style.

Analytics Over 2016-2022

The dashboard visualizes the correlation between two team statistics by plotting them against each other on the x and y-axis. Each team statistic is averaged across the chosen time period. Points are color coded by number of wins. Data can be filtered by year.
